My name is Sailaja Pallakonda, I am an automation engineer currently residing in Houston, TX.

I started my career in 2008 as a QC Analyst. I have had plenty of great opportunities to work for some good companies since then. Mostly, I am interested in webUI automation, but I try my best to be well rounded.

A few years back, after being part of a team for a few months, I was officially promoted to SME. I have found that I have just as much passion for guiding and building a good automation team as I have toward writing good software.

This site/blog focuses on my career as an automation engineer, but I should mention that my family and dog, are important parts of this site.
